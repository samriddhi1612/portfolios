Tachometer

Personal • Nov 2024 • Electronics · Arduino · Embedded C

Overview

IR reflective sensor + Arduino Uno. RPM measured via interrupts for precise pulse timing, displayed in real time. Built to validate drivetrain speeds and for embedded practice.

Highlights

  • Interrupt‑driven RPM measurement (stable at high speed)
  • Noise filtering on sensor input, debouncing as needed
  • Clear serial or LCD output options
